The Journey

Milestones &
Turning Points

The Beginning

Entering the Tri-Cities Market

Dawar begins his real estate career in Port Moody and the Tri-Cities — a market he chose deliberately for its character, its community, and its long-term fundamentals. From day one, his focus is on building relationships that outlast any single transaction.

Building the Practice

Founding Zada Group

As client volume and complexity grow, Dawar structures Zada Group — a specialist team operating under Stonehaus Realty Corp. Built around a simple premise: that clients deserve more expertise, more time, and more honesty than most real estate teams provide.

PREC Designation

Dawar Zada Personal Real Estate Corporation

Dawar incorporates his practice — a milestone that reflects both the depth of his production and his commitment to operating with the same professional rigour he expects of any specialist he recommends to his clients.

Master Medallion Club

Top-Tier Recognition

Dawar earns Master Medallion Club status through the Real Estate Board of Greater Vancouver — recognition reserved for REALTORS® who consistently rank among the most productive in the province. It is an acknowledgment of the work, not the point of it.

BC Bill 44 · SSMUH

Launching Port Moody Multiplex

When BC passes its Small-Scale Multi-Unit Housing legislation, Dawar sees the significance immediately. He builds portmoodymultiplex.ca — a specialist practice dedicated entirely to helping homeowners understand and act on this once-in-a-generation shift in residential land use.

Today

Advocacy, Education, and Execution

Dawar continues to operate at the intersection of real estate expertise and housing policy — advising homeowners across Port Moody's nine key neighbourhoods on their options under the new legislation, while staying active in the broader conversation around missing middle housing and urban density.
