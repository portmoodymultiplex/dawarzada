Dawar Zada

PREC  ·  Team Lead  ·  REALTOR®
Master Medallion Club MRP SRES Bill 44 Specialist

Dawar is the founding force of Zada Group and the architect of Port Moody Multiplex — his specialized practice built around BC's Bill 44 SSMUH legislation. With over a decade embedded in the Tri-Cities market, he brings a depth of local knowledge that simply isn't replicable. His Master Medallion Club recognition places him among the most consistently productive REALTORS® in the province — but what sets him apart is not volume. It is the quality of his counsel, and the time he takes to understand each homeowner's complete situation before offering a single recommendation.

Dawar holds his PREC (Personal Real Estate Corporation) designation and operates under Stonehaus Realty Corp. in Coquitlam, BC.

What Drives Us

We are not a regional team with a Port Moody desk. We live here, work here, and understand the streets, the soil conditions, the neighbourhood dynamics, and the planning history that shapes every decision.
